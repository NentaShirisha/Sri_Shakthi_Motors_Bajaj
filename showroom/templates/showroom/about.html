Our Story

Founded in 2010, Sri Shakthi Motors Bajaj began with a three-bay facility on the Hyderabad–Srisailam highway. Fifteen years later, we deliver 400+ bikes every quarter and support thousands of happy Bajaj riders across Ranga Reddy district.
